Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

How we handle your information and protect your identity.

1. Authentication & Identity

ThreadScout uses OpenID Connect (OIDC) for all user authentication. We do not manage or store your passwords directly. When you log in using an external provider (such as PocketID, Google, or eBay), we only receive and store the following information to identify your account:

  • Email Address: Used as your primary account identifier and for critical notifications.
  • Display Name: Your name as provided by your identity provider, used to personalize the dashboard.

2. Data Sovereignty

ThreadScout is built on the principle of Local-First Data.

  • Your product photographs are stored locally or in your designated S3-compatible bucket.
  • Sensitive integration tokens (like eBay OAuth tokens) are encrypted at rest using Fernet AES-256 encryption.
  • We do not sell your data or share your inventory information with anyone.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide automation features, ThreadScout interacts with specific third-party APIs:

AI Analysis (Google Gemini)

Selected images are sent to Google Vertex AI for visual attribute extraction.

Marketplace Integration (eBay)

We interact with the eBay API to generate drafts and perform price research on your behalf.

For security-related questions or data deletion requests, please refer to the project documentation on GitHub or contact support through the official channels.
