About ThreadScout

The dashing personal agent for high-velocity clothing resellers.

Our Mission: Accelerating the Reselling Workflow

ThreadScout is a localized automation tool designed specifically for clothing resellers. It bridges the gap between raw product photography and a completed eBay draft by leveraging advanced AI and automated market research. Our mission is to reduce the “time-to-list” from minutes to seconds.

Key Capabilities

AI Visual Extraction

Powered by Google Gemini 2.0 Flash to identify brand, size, material, and condition directly from your photos.

Market Enrichment

Integrated “Paste & Scrape” workflow to fetch market comparables and MSRP data automatically.

Secure Local-First

Your data belongs to you. We use application-level encryption for sensitive tokens and store information locally.

Multi-Domain Ingress

Seamlessly transitions between local dev, squad sharing via Tailscale, and public Cloudflare tunnels.

Our Visual Integrity Guarantee: Transparency in AI

In an era of generative AI, we understand that trust is your most valuable asset. If a buyer claims an item isn’t as described, you need to be able to prove the condition of the garment was never “touched up” by a computer.

ThreadScout is built on a Non-Destructive Guarantee. We track every single modification made to your photos from the moment they are uploaded. To keep you safe and your buyers informed, we classify every image into one of three transparent categories:

1. Original (Authentic Capture)

These are your source photos. Aside from being resized to fit standard screen dimensions, not a single pixel has been changed. What you see is exactly what was captured by your camera.

2. Studio Enhanced (Clean Backgrounds)

To help your items stand out, we use AI to “mask out” distracting backgrounds (like floors or cluttered rooms) and replace them with a clean studio finish. We guarantee that the pixels representing the garment itself are never altered. The item’s texture, color, and condition remain 100% authentic.

3. AI Visualization (Hero Shots)

Sometimes you want to show how a garment hangs or fits. For these “Hero” shots, we use advanced AI to reconstruct the item on a digital mannequin or person. Because these images involve computer-generated elements, they are always clearly labeled as “AI Visualization.” These are intended for search appeal, while your gallery remains the source of truth for condition.

Your Paper Trail (C2PA): We keep a permanent cryptographic record of these transformations using the C2PA (Content Credentials) industry standard. This allows you, your buyers, and marketplace investigators to independently verify the history of any image without simply having to take our word for it. If you ever need to verify the authenticity of a photo for a marketplace dispute, the data is embedded directly in the image and available for audit.
